Things you should know before startup

  1. Your ideas

    The main important thing before starting your startup is your ideas. Your ideas should be serviciable and acessiable to the people. If your ideas cannot fulfill the needs of people no matter how much you get funding from VC and ANGLES,it would be like pouring water into sands.The thing is your ideas should fulfill the gap needs of costumer. If your ideas is better, you can get funding from investor and your startup can grow larger.

  2. Funding

    When you start some project as your startup, you need money to invest in the project. If your ideas is capable of impressing the investors then the invester invest their money on your projects. Investors are of two types:

    1. VC
      2. VC are the investor that collect money from public like offering IPO(Initial public offering) and after collecting, they invest those money in your projects.
    2. Angles
      3. Angles are those investors that directly invest their money on your projects. They have huge amount of budjet so they can invest money in any project they like.

  3. Creating team

    4. Creating team is the toughest thing to do while doing startup. finding the person expert in particular sector is another burden. Team includes all sector person like lawer,marketer,advetiser,developer,designer etc.

  4. Team work

    5. Team work plays vital role in inceasing the company reputation and worth.No single person can grow the startup. If you think of going solo in startup without teamwork,you are dead wrong.

  5. Testing the product

    6. When you create the new product , test the product firstly and give your products to your friends ,family for testing and take a review about it. If the product is alright ,you can launch it .

  6. Legal adviser

    7. As your company grows,you have to pay tax like service and protection tax. Many paperworks,legal works should be done so their legal adviser comes in handy.

  7. Trust and Friendship

    8. Sometimes you cannot take decision on your own. Trusting and letting your team take decision sometimes may work. So trust and friendship is needed most.

  8. Demands of customer

    9. Reviewing your products by customer helps you to build product as your customer needs.Customer satisfacation can be acheived by reviewing your products by public.
